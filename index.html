ABOUT ME

FILLER BIO: I'm a multidisciplinary artist and designer based in New York City, working at the intersection of form, function, and emotion. With a background in ceramics and a passion for storytelling through material, I create pieces that blend utility with beauty. Whether shaping clay or coding websites, I strive to craft experiences that are both thoughtful and tactile. When I’m not in the studio, you’ll find me exploring galleries, sketching in cafés, or experimenting with new mediums.
